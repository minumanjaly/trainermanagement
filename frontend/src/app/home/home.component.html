What is ICT Academy of Kerala?

ICT Academy of Kerala is a Social Enterprise created in a Public Private Partnership model (PPP) for imparting ICT skills to the youths of Kerala and improve their employability opportunities in the Industry. The Company is supported by Govt. of India , partnered by Govt. of Kerala and the IT industry.

Our Key Focus Areas

Industry focused enablement programmes for Faculty members Industry focused ICT Skills development programmes for Students Project assignments related to capability building for Institutions and Corporate. Creating Intellectual Property on niche skill areas and emerging technologies.

ICTAK TRAINER MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Numerous organizations are now facing the major challenge to meet the training needs to train resource by assuring the standardized process of content development and delivery. Also, there is a necessity of using a consistent technology environment to meet the requirement. All training should be more sophisticated, flexible and scalable. So, the organizations require a solution for Planning, Designing Developing, Implementing and Evaluating the Learning Process. We have an in-depth domain of expertise, and we provide custom Learning Management solutions. Our certified experts in the field are capable of designing the system based on learning technology standards such as SCORM (Sharable Courseware Object Reference Model), AICC (Aviation Industry Computer-based Training Committee), IMS (Integrated Management Systems).
